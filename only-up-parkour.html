💡 Pro Vertical Navigation Rules

Resist the urge to constantly sprint. Sprinting significantly increases your character's sliding braking frames upon landing. Switch back to a regular jog or tap backward inputs immediately when landing on tiny pipes to stop your forward momentum instantly.

⛔ High-Risk Platforming Traps

The absolute biggest mistake is jumping blindly around tight structural corners without checking the camera first. Hidden rotating obstacles or narrow rails can easily deflect your hitbox outward and cause a complete vertical drop.
