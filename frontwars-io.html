🏃 Deployment and Control Guide

Spawn into your team's starting sector. Use your movement keys to navigate across the battlefield toward uncaptured gray nodes. Stand within the capture zones to claim them, generating a steady stream of points while deploying defensive guards to fortify your frontlines against incoming enemy pushes.

🎛️ Tactical Control Scheme

WASD / Arrow Keys: Guide your character or squad directions

Left Click / Tap: Target enemy units and issue fire commands

Numeric Keys (1-5): Hotkey selection for building reinforcements

Spacebar: Activate tactical movement dash or shield ability

💡 Pro Combat Zone Tactics
